Here are some of my Projects

  • PayNia

    Figma, Balsamic

    PayNia, a testament to my UX skills, is a mobile application designed to streamline shared expense management among groups, such as housemates or friends. With a focus on user-centric design, PayNia offers intuitive features like effortless bill splitting, comprehensive expense tracking, and seamless payment management. By incorporating user feedback and employing best practices in UX design, PayNia ensures a smooth and efficient experience for its users, showcasing my ability to create user-friendly and impactful digital solutions.

  • Muafakat Website

    Figma, HTML, CSS

    I designed and developed the official website for the Muafakat Games 2024, an annual sports event organized by Malaysian students in Australia. This event, which fosters unity and camaraderie among students and alumni from diverse backgrounds, receives direct support from Education Malaysia Australia and the High Commissioner. Using Figma for design and HTML and CSS for development, I created a visually appealing and user-friendly platform that captures the spirit of the games and provides essential information for participants and supporters.

  • Movie Plug

    Figma, HTML, CSS, JavaScript

    MoviePlug is a dynamic and user-friendly web application that allows users to effortlessly search for movies and explore detailed information about them. Built using vanilla JavaScript, the website leverages API integration to deliver real-time movie data, including summaries, ratings, and more. The clean, intuitive interface ensures an engaging user experience, making it a valuable resource for movie enthusiasts. This project showcases my ability to develop responsive, API-driven web applications from scratch.

  • Scoop for Justice

    Adobe Illustrator, Adobe Photoshop

    For this project, I crafted a thoughtful Instagram campaign for Mercy Malaysia in collaboration with Ben & Jerry's, designed to raise awareness and funds for humanitarian aid in Gaza. Utilizing Adobe Illustrator and Photoshop, I developed a series of impactful tiles that integrate Ben & Jerry's recognizable branding with urgent, sensitive messaging about the crisis in Rafah. The campaign introduces a new pistachio flavor, connecting to Palestinian culture, and includes a clear call to action: encouraging viewers to purchase the flavor to directly support humanitarian efforts. By carefully balancing engaging visuals with respectful, culturally aware content, the campaign is both compelling and mindful of the gravity of the issue.

  • Semporna Destination Website

    Figma, Html, CSS

    The Semporna website showcases the beauty and adventure of Sabah, Malaysia. From world-class diving spots like Sipadan to the serene luxury resorts of Mataking and Kapalai, it offers a glimpse into the diverse experiences Semporna offers. Whether diving among turtles and coral reefs or enjoying pristine beaches, Semporna promises an unforgettable escape.
